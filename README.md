# Ephemeral Voice

## About

This app lets you record and play back audio without saving files to your computer. It is designed for temporary use cases, such as voice or music training, where you want to hear yourself but don't need to keep the recording. If you need to save or edit your recordings, this app is not for you.

Whether you are perfecting a cartoon voice, rehearsing for a play, or simply working to feel more comfortable in your own voice, listening to yourself can be difficult. Trust me, I get it. For some people, this rings especially true. Don't give up. If you keep working at it, even just a little every day, you can do it. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new, even silly, things. Be proud of your progress and the effort you put in.

## User Guide

The app interface is simple, featuring four control buttons and three operating modes. The first three buttons represent the current mode: Recording, Stopped, and Playback. The active mode is indicated by the highlighted button.

- **To Record**: Press the microphone button (you will need to grant microphone access). The recording duration will count up. Note that new recordings will overwrite any previous ones.
- **To Play**: Press the play button. A progress bar across the screen shows your position in the recording.
- **To Stop**: Press the stop button. If you stop during playback, the position will reset to the start.
- **Loop Mode**: The far-right button toggles between looping the recording and playing it just once. Press it to switch modes.

No data is collected or saved externally. That's it. No data is collected, and no data will be collected.
